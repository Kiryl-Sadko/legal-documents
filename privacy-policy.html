Privacy Policy of Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds

At KIRYL SADKO, also known as CodeSculptors, we believe that transparency is the key to any healthy relationship. We appreciate that you trust us with information about your use of Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds, and we want to be transparent about how we use it.

General Information about Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds

When using Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds, no personal data is stored or collected by us. Our app does not offer account creation, nor does it collect personal information such as your age, gender, or contact details. The app records sounds during sleep, but these recordings are stored locally on your device, and we do not have access to these recordings.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Notifications

Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds uses notifications to remind you about your bedtime, alarm, and when the sleep recording (microphone) starts. These notifications are essential for the core functionality of the app.

Local Data Storage

All sleep sound recordings are stored locally on your device. Information about sleep sessions tracked by the user is also stored locally on the device.

User Rights

Users have full access to their information stored on their device. Since we do not store any personal data, there is no need for users to request data deletion or correction from us.

Customer Support

If you encounter any issues with Sleep Tracker: Sleep Sounds, you can contact our customer support at feedback.codesculptors@gmail.com. We may process minimal technical data (e.g., error reports) to resolve your issue. However, no personal data will be collected or processed.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. However, we use Firebase for collecting analytics data to improve app performance and functionality.

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will inform you of any changes by posting the updated policy in the app and on our website. If we make any material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email (if you have provided it).

Contact Information

If you have any questions or need further information about this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please contact us at feedback.codesculptors@gmail.com.
